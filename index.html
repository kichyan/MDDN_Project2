The online identity is a fake identity people build on the web. We will never know who he or she is behind the web. THE ONLINE SOCIETY

The online society is made up of various online application and communication system. These applications provide a fictitious online platform for people to make friend, post blog, record their life. This kind of application can be categorized into various type. Facebook, Tencent QQ, WeChat, this kind of app is designed for people to contact each other during the daily life. Twitter, Weibo, Tumblr, they are designed for people to post blog to show their talent, daily life, and also a common way for business to promote their product. The next type is for people who has similar interest people to make friends.
